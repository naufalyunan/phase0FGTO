Nano TIA: Leading the Future of Journalism


Founded in 2024, Nano TIA has grown to become a trusted name in the media industry, known for its integrity, excellence, and commitment to innovation.

Nano TIA has been recognized as the Best Journalism Company in Indonesia by JournalismToday from 2015 to 2023.

At Nano TIA, we are committed to delivering high-quality, reliable, and insightful news to our readers. Our team of experienced journalists and editors work tirelessly to cover a wide range of topics, from breaking news and current events to in-depth analyses and investigative reporting.

Nano TIA is proud to have been named the Best Journalism Company in Indonesia by JournalismToday from 2015 to 2023, a testament to our unwavering commitment to journalistic excellence. Join us on our journey as we continue to set the standard for journalism in Indonesia and beyond.

Extensive Distribution Network


Nano TIA has service offices spread across major cities in Indonesia and also in Singapore (Nano TIA Pte. Ltd./NTPL).


Nano TIA is the first local Indonesian journalism company to have a regional market reach in Asia through NTPL.

Vision

To be the most trusted and influential source of news and information in Indonesia and the Asia-Pacific region, empowering communities through high-quality journalism.

Mission

  • Deliver Accurate and Timely News: Provide our audience with the most accurate and timely information, keeping them informed about the latest developments locally and globally.
  • Uphold Journalistic Integrity: Maintain the highest standards of ethics and integrity in all our reporting, ensuring unbiased and fair journalism.
  • Innovate in Digital Journalism: Leverage cutting-edge technology to enhance our reporting, engage our audience, and adapt to the rapidly changing media landscape.
  • Foster Public Discourse: Encourage informed public discourse and debate, helping to build a more informed and engaged society.
  • Support Local and Regional Issues: Focus on issues that matter most to our local and regional communities, giving a voice to the underrepresented and marginalized.

Values

  • Integrity

    We are committed to honesty, transparency, and ethical journalism. Our integrity is the foundation of the trust our readers place in us.

  • Accountability

    We hold ourselves accountable to our audience, ensuring that our reporting is accurate, fair, and responsibly sourced.

  • Innovation

    We embrace innovation and continuously seek new ways to improve our journalism and engage with our audience.

  • Inclusivity

    We believe in the power of diverse perspectives and are committed to fostering an inclusive environment where all voices are heard.

  • Public Service

    We are dedicated to serving the public interest, providing information that empowers our readers to make informed decisions and participate fully in society.
